Our Story

In India, most local businesses don't have a social media page, so they miss out on hundreds of leads. We started Novant Media to fix this. We are two founders who are serious about helping you get disciplined, sustainable growth.

Data-Driven Strategy

We analyze your brand, build a custom plan (AIDA, SWOT), and optimize hashtags & timing to find your perfect audience.

Content & Video Production

Our team handles everything: recording, editing, and writing scroll-stopping posts, ads, and videos that get results.

Full Social Management

We manage your entire page, run engagement systems, and deliver monthly reports so you can focus on your business.

Meet the Growth Experts

Meet the minds behind Novant Media. We're a team of founders and experts dedicated to your growth.

R

Raghav

Founder

"Lead Visionary"

Raghav is a 17-year-old entrepreneur who always felt more drawn to building things than to traditional academics. Growing up with a mother who is a professor, it was almost ironic—he lived in a home that valued education deeply, yet he never connected with studies the way he connected with ideas. What inspired him was not textbooks, but the thought of creating something meaningful. From a young age, he believed that real change happens when someone decides to solve a problem instead of waiting for others to do it. That mindset led him to start his agency on 1 November 2025 with one clear purpose: to help hidden and hardworking brands get the visibility they deserve. He saw that many great businesses stay unnoticed not because they lack quality, but because no one knows their story. Being underestimated for his age only pushed him harder. He made a quiet promise to himself that he would build something impactful before turning 18—something that proved passion can be stronger than expectations. Today, his vision is focused and ambitious: to grow across India and become one of the top social media agencies built on honesty, results, and genuine care for clients. For Raghav, this agency is not just a project. It is the start of a lifelong mission to bring deserving brands into the spotlight.
